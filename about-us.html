About Creative Guild Games

Independent Game Studio

Creative Guild LLC is a small, freelance-driven game studio focused on building immersive, engaging gaming experiences with strong visual identity.

Founded on the idea of artistic collaboration, we bring together developers, artists, writers, and audio designers to create games that feel hand-crafted and personal.

Our Values

Collaboration First

We prioritize healthy, sustainable collaboration with freelancers and partners across the games industry.

Player-Focused Design

Every mechanic, system, and visual choice is filtered through the lens of player experience and clarity.

Art-Forward Worlds

We aim for strong visual identities that make each project instantly recognizable and memorable.
